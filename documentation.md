## Operators

- `+` Addition
- `-` Subtraction
- `*` Multiplication
- `/` Division
- `^` Exponentiation

## Keywords

- `let` -> Assign variables
- `true`|`false` = 1 | 0
- `if` | `else` | `then` | `else if` -> conditions
- `for` | `while` | `then` -> loops
- `inc` -> Define increment of variable in for loop  (Optional)

### Rewords

- expr -> exp
- step -> inc
