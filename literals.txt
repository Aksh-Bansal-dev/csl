Priority Seq: factor > term > expr

expr    : term ( (+ | -) term ) *

term    : factor ( (* | /) factor) *

factor  : (+ | -) factor
        : power

power   : atom (POW factor)*

atom    : INT/FLOAT
        : LPAR expr RPAR (eg: (2+3) would be a factor)

* means they could have multiple or no +- inside
(factor can have only one number therefore no *)
